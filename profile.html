PAKISTAN

Pakistan is a country located in South Asia. It is divided mainly into four provinces: Punjab, Sindh, Balochistan, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. Each province has their own customs and traditions. Each province has its own language but the national language is URDU. Pakistan is so diverse but still people are united as a nation and celebrate each festival and our culture together.

Fascinating Facts
  • Pakistan has World's Largest Voluneer Ambulance Service
  • K2 Mountain which is the world's second largest peak is located in Pakistan
  • World's Second Largest Salt Mine is in here named Khewra Mines
  • The world's famous pink Himalayan Salt is also mined in Pakistan
  • Center of the production of Soccer Balls
  • Pakistan has the world's fourth(4th)largest irrigation system
  • The highest Polo ground in the world is in Shandur, Pakistan
CUISINE

Pakistani cuisine is a blend of different regional cooking styles and traditions. Pakistani cuisine is known for its spice blends.
